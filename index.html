SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is the process of improving a website's visibility on search engines like Google. By optimizing various elements of a site, such as keywords, content, and technical aspects, SEO helps increase the site's chances of ranking higher in search results. This can drive more organic (non-paid) traffic to the site, improving its chances of reaching potential users or customers.

Google Lighthouse is an open-source tool used to evaluate the performance, accessibility, and SEO of web pages. It provides detailed reports and actionable insights on various aspects of a website, such as loading speed, mobile responsiveness, and best practices for search engine optimization. By running audits with Lighthouse, developers and site owners can identify areas for improvement and enhance the overall user experience.
